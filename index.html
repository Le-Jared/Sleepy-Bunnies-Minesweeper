Sleepy Bunnies

Instructions

Use the left click button on the mouse to select a space on the grid. If you hit a bunny, you wake it up and lose.

You can right click or hold a square (on mobile) with the mouse to place a carrot where you think a bunny is. This allows you to avoid that spot.

The numbers on the board represent how many bunnies are adjacent to a square. For example, if a square has a "3" on it, then there are 3 bunnies next to that square. The bunnies could be above, below, right left, or diagonal to the square.

Avoid all the bunnies and expose all the empty spaces to win Sleepy Bunnies.

Tip: Use the numbers to determine where you know a bunny is.
